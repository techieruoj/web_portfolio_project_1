About Me

Dance and technology are central to who I am, providing avenues for connection and expression. I'm dedicated to creating accessible dance experiences and have played a key role in Dance Parade, producing NYC’s largest dance event and advocating for dance freedom. My work involves strategic advocacy, impactful social media campaigns, and engaging festival programming to celebrate movement. I’m currently learning web development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) at The Knowledge House to build the digital infrastructure for my innovative ideas. This technical foundation will help bring my vision for inclusive dance experiences to life.
